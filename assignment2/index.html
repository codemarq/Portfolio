John-Mike is an aspiring designer and front-end web developer located in Durham, NC. Originally from New York, John-Mike moved down to the triangle in 2008 and immediately fell in love with the area. He met his wife, bought a home (no more renting a tiny apartment) and started a family. First came the family dog, Autumn--the laziest Jack Russel Terrier on the planet--and then his very energetic son, Dax, who makes John-Mike the happiest dad in the world.
